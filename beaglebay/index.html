BeagleBay

A wellness companion built specifically for beagles.

Beagles aren't just dogs — they're hounds. They need more sniff time, more movement, and more mental work than the average pup. BeagleBay is designed around that reality, not retrofitted from a generic pet app.

What it does

  • GPS walk tracker — live route map, distance, pace, and elevation.
  • Wellness log — weight, meals, vet visits, grooming and training notes, with trend charts.
  • Breed-specific tips — activity, nutrition, training, health and safety, all tuned for beagles.
  • Vocalisation insights (optional) — counts your beagle's bays and howls during walks using on-device sound recognition.
  • Apple Health — reads your steps and active calories; writes finished walks back to Apple Health.
  • Smart reminders — daily walk nudge that skips itself when you've already walked.

Privacy first

  • No ads. No trackers. No analytics SDKs.
  • No in-app purchases. No subscriptions.
  • Audio for vocalisation detection is processed on-device and never recorded or uploaded.
  • Export your data as JSON or delete your account in two taps.
