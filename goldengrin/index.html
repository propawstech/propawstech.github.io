GoldenGrin

A wellness app made just for golden retrievers — by people who came home to the same big smile every day.

Goldens aren't just dogs. They need their walks to count, their ears dried after every swim, their joints kept gentle, and their lumps watched. GoldenGrin is designed around that reality, not retrofitted from a generic pet app.

What it does

  • GPS walk tracker — live route map, distance, pace, elevation, with Sniff / Exercise / Training tags so a swim or nose-work session counts the way it should.
  • Heat & humidity walk-safety advisory — humidity-aware caution thresholds plus the vet-recommended pavement five-second rule.
  • Wellness log — weight, meals, vet visits, grooming and training notes, with trend charts and a food-safety lookup.
  • Routine ear-check log — one-tap reminder + log, perfect for water-loving floppy-eared breeds.
  • Seizure event log — a built-in stopwatch, date, duration, and notes for owners managing epilepsy.
  • "Share with vet" PDF — a clean multi-page summary you can AirDrop or email to your vet before any visit.
  • Breed-specific tips — activity, nutrition, training, health and safety, all tuned for golden retrievers.
  • Vocal moments (optional) — counts your golden's barks during walks using on-device sound recognition.
  • Apple Health — reads your steps and active calories; writes finished walks back to Apple Health.

Privacy first

  • No ads. No trackers. No analytics SDKs.
  • No in-app purchases. No subscriptions.
  • Audio for vocalisation detection is processed on-device and never recorded or uploaded.
  • Walks, weight, meals, ear checks, seizure events, photos — all stored on your device only. The vet PDF is the only way any of it leaves the phone, and you choose when.
  • Export your data or delete your account in two taps.
